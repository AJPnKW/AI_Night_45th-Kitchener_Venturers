Before the session

Default plan: arrive with a browser-ready device if available. No VM is required by default.

  • Charge your device and bring the charger.
  • If your family is comfortable, sign in to a browser-based AI tool before arrival.
  • If you do not have a device or cannot sign in, you can still participate through the fallback path.
  • Bring one topic you care about: school, hobbies, planning, stories, art, or event ideas.
