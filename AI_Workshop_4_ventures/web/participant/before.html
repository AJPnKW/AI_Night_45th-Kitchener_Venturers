Before the session

Get ready in a few simple checks.

You do not need to over-prepare. Just make sure the basics are ready so the session feels smoother.

Fast checklist

  • Know the date, start time, and location from the final reminder.
  • Location: Hope Lutheran Pres Church, 30 Shaftsbury.
  • Bring your laptop or Chromebook and charger.
  • Do the readiness check if you still need setup help.
  • Ask a parent or guardian to review the approval page if needed.

What to bring

  • A laptop or Chromebook.
  • A charger.
  • A notebook if you like writing ideas down.
  • A simple project idea you care about.
Open readiness check

Safe-use rules

  • Do not share private personal information.
  • Do not paste passwords, addresses, or health details into AI tools.
  • If the AI gives a surprising answer, ask an adult or verify it.
  • Keep prompts respectful and event-appropriate.

Good starting idea

Think of one thing you want help with: learning, planning, writing, brainstorming, or making a small project concept better.
