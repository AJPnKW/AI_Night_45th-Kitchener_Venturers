Venturer sign-up and readiness workflow.

This page covers the real participant workflow: sign-up, age on the night of the event, safe-use agreement, readiness for devices and ChatGPT, and a submission path the organizer can use to keep track of completed forms.

Event context: age is measured on April 14, 2026. This form does not ask for birthdate. It only asks for the participant's age on the event night.

What this workflow does

  • Records the participant's first name and age on April 14, 2026
  • Checks device and ChatGPT readiness
  • Confirms the participant understands the AI-use rules
  • Shows whether parent approval is likely required
  • Lets the form be submitted to the organizer inbox when live collection is turned on
  • Still supports local save, download, print, or save-as-PDF as a fallback

What happens next
