After the session

Keep the useful parts, not the overwhelm.

You do not need to become an expert in one night. The goal is to notice what helped, what felt unclear, and what you want to practice next.

Three good wrap-up questions

  • What kind of prompt gave me the best result?
  • When did I realize the AI needed checking?
  • What small project could I keep improving?

Save carefully

Keep useful notes, prompts, or project text. Do not save personal data in shared places.

Try one more version

Improvement usually comes from a second or third prompt, not the first try.

Ask for help

If you want to keep learning, ask a leader or trusted adult what safe next step makes sense.
