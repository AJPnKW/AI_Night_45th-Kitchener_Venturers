Youth: What to Expect

Simple browser-first guide for youth participants.

What to expect

You do not need to be a coder. Tonight is about clearer prompts, practical browser use, and noticing what changes when you ask more clearly.

What to bring

  • Device if you have one
  • Charger or power cable
  • Optional Ethernet cable if you have one
  • One topic you care about

How the session works

You will watch short demos, try guided versions yourself, and save or print a reflection report before you leave.

Your workbook path

Your participant workbook is meant to feel like your own learning booklet for the night. Use it to choose an activity, record your notes, capture what changed, and keep your takeaways.

How your workbook works

Your notes stay on this device unless you choose to print them, save them as PDF, or download a copy.

If you get stuck

  • Use Guided.
  • Borrow a starter card.
  • Add one detail at a time.
  • Ask a leader or the facilitator for a sentence frame.
