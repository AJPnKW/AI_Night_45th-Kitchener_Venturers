Project building

Pick something small enough to finish one useful version.

A good beginner project is clear, personal, and realistic in one session. You can always improve it later.

Starter structure

Pick a topic, decide the output, make the prompt specific, review the answer, then revise once or twice.

Study idea

Ask for a quiz, summary, or revision checklist.

Creative idea

Ask for a story concept, game pitch, or character profile.

Life-planning idea

Ask for a routine, event checklist, or simple meal plan.

Prompt pattern

“Help me create a [type of output] about [topic] for [audience]. Use [format]. Keep it [tone or limit].”

Project review questions

  • Does the result match what I asked for?
  • Is anything obviously wrong or weird?
  • What one detail would improve it next?

Good next step

If your first result is messy, that is normal. Ask the AI to shorten, simplify, reformat, or explain one part at a time.
