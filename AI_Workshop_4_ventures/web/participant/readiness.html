Participant readiness

Quick check before the workshop.

This readiness check helps the facilitator and leaders know who is ready to log in, who needs setup help, and what participants want to explore. It is only for workshop preparation.

Event snapshot: Venturer AI and ChatGPT workshop, Hope Lutheran Pres Church, 30 Shaftsbury, Wi-Fi available, participants bring a laptop or Chromebook, and support is available for mixed skill levels.

What this asks

  • Your first name
  • Whether your device is ready
  • Whether you can access ChatGPT or still need setup help
  • Your current comfort level
  • What you want to build or explore
