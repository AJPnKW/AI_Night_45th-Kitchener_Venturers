Prompt examples

Simple examples you can copy and improve.

These examples are meant to be changed. The best version is usually the one you customize.

Remember

Start small, then revise. A clear second draft is usually better than a vague first try.

Study help

“Explain photosynthesis to a 16-year-old in 5 bullets and one simple analogy.”

Creative writing

“Give me 3 story ideas about a camp mystery, each with a different mood.”

Planning

“Make a simple weekend plan with indoor and outdoor options and a low budget.”

How to improve a prompt

  • Add the audience.
  • Ask for a format.
  • Set a length or limit.
  • Say what tone you want.

What not to paste

  • Passwords
  • Home address
  • Medical details
  • Private conversations
