Short answers to common workshop questions.

FAQ

Do I need a VM?

No. Browser-first is the default participant path.

Do I need coding?

No. The session is about prompting, comparison, and reflection.

Where do workbook notes go?

Workbook notes stay in the browser unless the learner prints or downloads them.

How are surveys handled?

Structured surveys and feedback use organizer-issued Google Forms rather than GitHub Pages.

AI Night

Guided browser-based introduction to AI for Scouts and Venturers. The main outcomes are safer use, clearer prompts, reflection, and practical confidence.
