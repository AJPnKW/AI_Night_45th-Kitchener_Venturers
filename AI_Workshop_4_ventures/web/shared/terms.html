Plain language glossary

Simple words for common AI terms.

Use this if a term feels confusing. These are practical event-friendly definitions, not academic ones.

Most useful reminder

AI output can be helpful without being fully correct. “Useful” and “true” are not the same thing.

Prompt

The instruction or question you give an AI tool.

AI tool

The app or website a person uses, like ChatGPT.

Model

The underlying system that predicts patterns and generates output.

Hallucination

When the AI gives an answer that sounds believable but is wrong or invented.

Verification

Checking important information using another source or a human review.

Revision

Improving the prompt or result after the first try.
