Planner Notes

Best Live Hands-On Source

Teachable Machine is the strongest no-code entry point for a 90–120 minute youth session.

Best Classroom Structure

Code.org provides the most classroom-ready mix of lesson plans, assessments, and teacher supports.

Best Extension Worksheets

Machine Learning for Kids provides downloadable student worksheets and project structures.

Best Teacher Context

OpenAI Teaching with AI and ISTE AI Lessons help frame responsible and practical use.
