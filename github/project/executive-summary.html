Project Executive Summary

One-page portfolio summary of the project, its scope, value, PM approach, and outcomes.

Project in one paragraph

This project created a browser-first AI Night delivery system for Scouts and Venturers, including public role-based pages, participant exercises, reflections, knowledge pages, facilitator materials, validation, packaging, and a project-light governance layer. It began as a personal volunteer proof of concept in an unfamiliar technical space and matured through investigation, architecture correction, rationalization, and production hardening into a coherent validated system.

Why this matters in a PM portfolio

  • Shows right-sizing instead of process overkill.
  • Shows scope control and evidence-based correction of weak assumptions.
  • Shows stakeholder-aware content and operational planning.
  • Shows repeatable delivery through build, validation, packaging, and status evidence.
