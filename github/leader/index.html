Leader support for the same shared workshop plan.

Leaders are supporting one beginner-friendly AI and ChatGPT session for about 6 Venturers, with 3 scouters in the room, Wi-Fi available, participant laptops or Chromebooks, optional pre-homework, and quiet support for mixed ability learners including 2 neurodivergent youth.

Important: this is an independent volunteer-created support page, not an official unit or Scouts Canada operational system.

Shared session timeline

  • 1Short intro
  • 2Guided demo
  • 3Guided first task
  • 4Independent or small project work
  • 5Wrap-up and sharing

Before the Session

  • Confirm the room, seating, Wi-Fi, and charger access are ready at Hope Lutheran Pres Church, 30 Shaftsbury.
  • Confirm participants know to bring a laptop or Chromebook.
  • Review the same date, start time, and location reminder being sent to families.
  • Check whether any readiness responses suggest setup help is needed before the session starts.
  • Agree on who will handle greeting, supervision, and quiet support.

During the Session

  • Keep the room calm and the five-step sequence visible.
  • Watch for learners who need a clearer instruction or a reset.
  • Normalize quiet support, short breaks, and different working speeds.
  • Redirect unsafe data-sharing or off-track use early.

After the Session

  • Help with pack-up, check no equipment is left behind, and confirm follow-up needs.
  • Share any safeguarding, support, readiness, or logistics notes with the facilitator and organizer.
  • Keep communication consistent with the same workshop framing used on participant and facilitator pages.

Leader focus

  • Session purpose: beginner-friendly practical use of AI and ChatGPT.
  • Support model: visible supervision, clear communication, and low-pressure help.
  • Readiness awareness: know who still needs device or ChatGPT setup help.
  • Parent communication: be ready to point families to readiness, privacy, and consent pages.
  • Terminology: use the same words as the rest of the site: intro, demo, first task, project work, wrap-up.
