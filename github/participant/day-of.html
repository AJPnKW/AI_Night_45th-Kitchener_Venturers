During the session

What the evening will usually feel like.

The workshop happens at Hope Lutheran Pres Church, 30 Shaftsbury, and follows the same shared sequence everyone has been told about: short intro, guided demo, guided first task, project work, and wrap-up. You do not need to rush.

If you lose track

Ask: “Which step are we on?” The structure repeats on purpose so it is easier to rejoin.

Likely flow

  • 1Short intro
  • 2Guided demo
  • 3Guided first task
  • 4Project work with support
  • 5Wrap-up and sharing

Simple self-check

  • Do I know the current task?
  • Am I stuck on a small detail that I can ask about?
  • Can I make my prompt more specific?
  • Am I keeping private information out of prompts?

Prompt upgrade formula

Say the goal, who it is for, the output format, and any limits.

Example

“Help me make a one-page study guide about black holes for a 16-year-old, using simple words and 5 bullets.”

Reality check

AI can sound confident and still be wrong. Important answers still need human checking.
