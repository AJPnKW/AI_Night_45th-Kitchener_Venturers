Pre-work and pre-flight checklist.

This page is the final preparation page before AI Night. Use it to check devices, login readiness, safe-use rules, and what to bring so the evening starts smoothly.

Night-of snapshot

  • Date context: Tuesday, April 14, 2026
  • Location: Hope Lutheran Pres Church, 30 Shaftsbury
  • Bring: laptop or Chromebook and charger
  • Wi-Fi: available
  • Flow: intro, demo, first task, project, wrap-up
1

Finish sign-up

Complete the participant sign-up so leaders know your readiness and what you want to build.

2

Check account access

Make sure you can reach ChatGPT or know whether you still need help before the event.

3

Bring the right gear

Laptop or Chromebook, charger, and one simple idea you care about.

4

Remember the safety rules

Do not put passwords, home address, medical details, or other sensitive information into AI prompts.

Pre-work

Optional before-you-arrive prep

  • Think of one simple problem you want AI help with.
  • Try writing one question you would ask ChatGPT.
  • Read the sign-up summary if you saved it as a PDF or printed it.
  • Ask for help early if device or ChatGPT setup still feels unclear.

Pre-flight

Final checklist before leaving

  • Laptop or Chromebook packed
  • Charger packed
  • Know the location and expected start time
  • Parent waiver completed if required
  • One project idea in mind

Safe-use reminders

  • Do not share personal or sensitive information.
  • Do not paste passwords into AI tools.
  • Ask an adult or leader if an answer seems strange or risky.
  • Keep prompts respectful and workshop-appropriate.
