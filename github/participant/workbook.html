Participant Workbook: Start and Setup

Private-by-default participant workbook engine for the guided event flow.

Participant workbook model

This workbook is meant to feel like your guided learning notebook for the night. Use it to choose an activity, record what you tried, capture what changed, and keep the notes you want to take away with you.

  • Private by default: notes stay in this browser unless you print or download them.
  • Guided learning path: you can choose a level and category without starting from a blank page.
  • Follow-up coaching: the workbook should help you notice gaps and plan the next prompt improvement.
  • Fast-finisher support: if you move quickly, use the extension section instead of waiting.

Participant workbook flow

This is one workbook journey. The pages are separate only so the night feels calmer and easier to follow step by step.

Step 1

Start and setup (Current step)

Choose your topic, your level, and the kind of help you want.

Step 2

Step 3

Step 4

Review what changed, what still needs checking, and what you want to keep.

The workbook engine should keep your place and your notes in this browser.

How this workbook engine works

This workbook engine runs in the browser and saves your progress on this device as you move from page to page. That means you can leave one step, come back later on the same device, and pick up where you left off.

  • Your notes stay in this browser unless you print them, save them as PDF, or download them.
  • This workbook is meant to feel like one guide with multiple steps, not like separate disconnected tools.
  • If a Google Form workflow is added later, that would be the tracked organizer path. This browser workbook remains the private fallback path.

How to use this workbook

  • Start here and fill in only what you need to begin.
  • Move forward one step at a time instead of jumping between separate tools.
  • Let the workbook keep your notes in this browser as you progress.
  • Save, download, or print your workbook before you close the page.
