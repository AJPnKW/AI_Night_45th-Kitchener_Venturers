Ownership clarity

This website is independent and volunteer-created.

It supports a specific AI Coding Night for 1st Stanley Park Venture Company, but it is not an official Scouts Canada, group-owned, or unit-owned website.

What this means

  • The wording here is event support content, not official organizational policy.
  • Official logos are intentionally not required by this design.
  • Any future branding image should remain clearly framed as event support imagery.

Practical boundary

Families should treat this site as a volunteer information and coordination resource. Official organizational questions should still be directed through official channels.
