Frequently asked questions

Short answers for participants and families.

If the answer here is not enough, use the privacy, consent, or participant pages for more detail.

Fast answer

The event is meant to be practical, supervised, and low-pressure. The site that explains it is volunteer-run and independent.

Do participants need to already know coding?

No. The session is designed for beginners and focuses on guided use, prompting, and critical thinking.

Is ChatGPT always correct?

No. It can sound convincing and still be wrong, so important answers should be checked.

What data should participants avoid sharing?

Private personal details, passwords, home address, financial details, medical information, and anything they would not want copied elsewhere.

Is this an official 45th Kitchener Group or Scouts Canada website?

No. It is an independently created volunteer support site for one event.

How is approval handled?

Through a minimal consent process described on the consent page, with a static-site-compatible form option and a printable fallback path.
